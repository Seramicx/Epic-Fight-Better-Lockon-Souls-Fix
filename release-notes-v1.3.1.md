## 1.3.1

- Fixed attacks going off-target when sprinting + locked on + holding S+D or S+A.
- Fixed shoulder/overhead camera offset breaking when other mods inject fields onto vanilla `Camera`.
- Added [Bosses' Rise](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/bossesrise) compatibility: roll animations play, rolls follow W/A/S/D direction, camera stays decoupled mid-roll.

**Minecraft:** 1.20.1 Forge. **Requires:** Epic Fight + Better Lockon.
