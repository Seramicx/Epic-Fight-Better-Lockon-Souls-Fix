## 1.2.1

- **Iron's Spells Integration**: Your character now smoothly auto-faces enemies when casting spells while locked on (works for both held spells and instant casts).
- **Better Crosshair Aiming**: The third-person adaptive crosshair now accurately shows exactly where your shot or spell will land when drawing a bow, crossbow, trident, or channeling a spell.
- **Instant Spell Snapping**: Tapping an instant spell (like Teleport or Firebolt) in third-person will now instantly snap your character's aim to the crosshair, making sure the spell goes exactly where you're looking instead of getting messed up by the shoulder camera angle.
- **Camera Collision Fix**: Fixed a bug where the camera would violently clip through walls or jump around in tight spaces.
- **Underwater Fix**: Fixed the lock-on icon and camera flickering aggressively when fighting underwater.
- Tweaked the default over-the-shoulder camera offset for better alignment right out of the box.
- Cleaned up some old aiming code that was causing the crosshair to misbehave.

**Minecraft:** 1.20.1 Forge. **Requires:** Epic Fight (Better Lock On optional).
