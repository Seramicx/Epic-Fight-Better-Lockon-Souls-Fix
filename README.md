# Epic Fight x Better Lock On: Movement Fixes

A bunch of small fixes for using Epic Fight and Better Lockon together. Adds proper souls-style movement when you're locked on. Forge 1.20.1.

## What it does

- WASD moves you relative to the camera while locked on, instead of dragging you toward the enemy.
- Dodge rolls go in the direction you press, not always toward the target.
- Attack lunges actually hit the target.
- Bow, crossbow, and trident shots go where the crosshair is.
- Buckets, eggs, fishing rods, and Iron's Spells instant items use where you're aiming, not your body angle.
- Auto lock-on when you swing at an unlocked enemy.
- Mouse flick to switch targets (works with Controllable too).
- Body smoothly turns to face the target while blocking or casting.

## Optional integrations

Auto-detected if installed: Better Lockon, Iron's Spells, Bosses' Rise, FTB Teams, Controllable.
